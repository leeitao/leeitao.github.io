About US

Guangzhou Chengjin Technology Co., Ltd. is located in Baiyun District, Guangzhou. It is a manufacturer of automotive electronic products R&D, production, sales and service. The company specializes in automotive safety and focuses on automotive safety driving services. The main products include: Onboard camera, Onboard Loggers, on-board monitors, and taxi radars and other products. The company strictly controls the quality and strictly supervises the quality of raw materials, importing well-known brand LED TFT panels, chips, and programs. The quality of our products never shuts down. QC and QA departments have a two-pronged approach to provide customers with high-quality products.

Several aspects of product testing, such as: high temperature, low temperature test, waterproof test, wide voltage test, aging test, toxic substances detection and so on.

OEM/ODM services, customized services for different customers, such as replacement of product software, customized models, and so on.

Company products are exported to Germany, Britain, the United States, Italy, Brazil, Dubai, Europe and the United States and Southeast Asian countries. Due to the quality of our products, fast delivery process and competitive prices, our products enjoy a certain reputation in the market.
